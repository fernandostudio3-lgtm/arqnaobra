Design Inspiration Summary

This section outlines conceptual design inspirations for the "Arquiteto na Obra" website, based on the inferred professional and creative theme of architecture and construction.

General Aesthetic/Theme: Modern, professional, clean, and inspiring, emphasizing trust, quality, and innovative design. The tone should be authoritative yet approachable. Visuals should include high-quality project imagery, geometric shapes, clean lines, and ample negative space.

Proposed Layout Structure: A comprehensive layout including a fixed header, a prominent hero section, dedicated sections for services (grid/card), an interactive portfolio gallery with filtering, an "About Us" section detailing mission/vision/values, client testimonials in a carousel, and a contact section with a form and map integration. A robust footer with copyright and policy links completes the structure.

Proposed Color Palette (Conceptual): Main Neutrals: Charcoal grey (#333333) or deep navy blue (#1A2B3D) for primary, and off-white (#F8F8F8) or light grey (#EFEFEF) for secondary. Accent Colors: A refined choice of muted teal (#008C8C) or deep green (#2E8B57) for a modern, trustworthy feel; or burnt orange (#CC6600) or soft gold (#B8860B) for a warmer, inviting approach.

Proposed Typography (Conceptual): Headlines (H1, H2, H3): Strong, modern sans-serif fonts such as Montserrat, Open Sans, Lato, or Poppins. Body Text: Highly readable sans-serif fonts like Roboto, Noto Sans, or Source Sans Pro, or a subtle serif like Merriweather or Lora for added elegance. Accent/Interactive Elements: Utilizing the same sans-serif fonts as headlines/body but with varied weights or all caps for distinction in buttons and interactive components.
